How to Play

Classic property-trading rules, modern companies.

1
Roll and move. Roll two dice and walk the board. Doubles roll again — but three doubles in a row lands you in SEC Detention.
2
Buy companies. Land on an unowned company and you can buy it. Own every company in a sector and rents double.
3
Build it up. Open your Portfolio to build offices on full sectors (up to 4 levels — the 4th is the golden HQ). Rent grows fast.
4
Collect rent. Other players landing on your companies pay you. Logistics companies and cloud services have their own rent rules.
5
Survive. Pass IPO DAY for a salary. If you can't pay a debt, sell assets — or go bankrupt. Last player standing wins.
